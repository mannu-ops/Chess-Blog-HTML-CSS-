3rd President Cup R7: Nihal and Sindarov emerge coleaders, Abhimanyu forces draw against Abdusattorov

mannu |   Nov 12, 2024

GM Abhimanyu Puranik held the top seed, GM Nodirbek Abdusattorov (UZB) to a draw in Round 7 of 3rd President Cup 2024. The former forced a draw via perpetual check against the World no.6. GM Nihal Sarin scored a comfortable win over IM Ortik Nigmatov (UZB). Nihal is now coleader with GM Javokhir Sindarov (UZB) 6/7 each. Sindarov defeated GM Sankalp Gupta. IM Neelash Saha continues his great form. He had a solid draw against GM Parham Maghsoodloo (IRI). Neelash and GM Shamsiddin Vokhidov (UZB) are trailing the leaders by a half point at 5.5/7 each. IM Ameya Audi scored a fine victory over GM M Amin Tabatabaei (IRI). Round 8 starts today at 2 p.m. local time, 2:30 p.m. IST.

Neelash holds Parham to a solid draw, Ameya stuns Tabatabaei

GM Abhimanyu Puranik (2652) forced a draw via perpetual check against GM Nodirbek Abdusattorov (UZB, 2777).

Nihal - Ortik

IM Ortik Nigmatov (UZB, 2447) went wrong early in the opening 7...h6? GM Nihal Sarin (2676) does not miss these opportunities. It cost him a valuable pawn 8.Bxf6 Qxf6 9.Nxd5 Black cannot take the knight 9...cxd5 as White recovers material 10.Qxc8+ and gains another pawn in the following move Qxb7. Black could not get back for the rest of the game. White eventually gained one more pawn and went on to win the game.
